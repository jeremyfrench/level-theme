James Dobree

Founder and CEO

The idea for this business grew from the frustrations of my wife, who runs her own design business. One day she asked "Why can’t I put 'roof slate suppliers' into Google and get a list back based on how reliable they are?" The idea of providing businesses with information about each other, to make it easier to do business with each other was born.

There is so much information about businesses on the internet, but it's not organised and presented in a way to answer my wife’s question. Organise and present that information to help businesses trade better...now that is a bold vision.

With a great team and great backing we are building the best web application we can to deliver to businesses information they can use, free and without barriers, so that they can trade better, faster and more cheaply.
