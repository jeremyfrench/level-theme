Why is Level Business providing this information for free? Will I have to pay for it sometime in the future?

We believe in removing barriers to any business data that is valuable to users who need to know more about UK businesses in their daily work. We don't believe in charging for access to data and want to provide as much value as possible to users of this site without any fee, so that everyone can benefit. In time, we intend to build paid-for services on top of the data - but will always make the data itself freely available.

The business world is currently divided into the data rich and data poor. Data is sold by volume and most of it is out of the reach of smaller businesses. That is why we called our company 'level'. We want to create information equality so that everyone benefits. The unequal access to accurate, timely information wastes effort, drives poor decision making and creates friction across the UK economy.

What are Level Business's long term intentions?

We are building an increasingly valuable body of business information, from multiple sources, and we are making it as accessible and as useful as we possibly can. In time, we intend to provide multiple services, for different types of users, based on that information.

Why are my company accounts being made available to everyone?

The financial accounts we are publishing are already publicly available through Companies House. Level Business is simply making them more accessible to everyone, under a licence from Companies House, by removing the barriers to their use. Currently they are hidden behind a pay wall; users have to pay a fee and can wait up to 30 minutes for the download. Through levelbusiness.com they are searchable, immediately viewable and free. Not only will this save countless hours for the current users of the service, but it will encourage greater use of this important data.

Can I take my company data down if I want to?

Sorry, but no. That isn't possible. Businesses can not remove their financial data from public view at Companies House.

My company data is incorrect. What can I do?

We believe the company accounts shown on the site are accurate as they are a direct representation of what is already available at Companies House. However there is a Feedback button on every page and we encourage you to use this to report anything that you believe is incorrect.
